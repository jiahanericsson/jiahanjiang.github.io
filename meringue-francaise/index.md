layout: recipe
title: "meringue française (french meringue)"

---
### quantity
~600g

### ingredients
egg whites - 7 or 200g

granulated sugar - 200g

icing sugar - 200g

### process
sift icing sugar

whip the egg whites, adding granulated sugar in 3 batches: once at the beginning, once during mixing, and once towards the end

mix delicately the sifted icing sugar by hand with a pastry scraper

pipe into shape (such as the nest for pavlova), optionally sprinkle with sugar, and bake at 100°C maximum
